Emperor Tenmu

Hello! Here, we will introduce you to Emperor Tenmu, the kami enshrined here.

Who is Emperor Tenmu?

Emperor Tenmu was the 40th Emperor of Japan, a historical figure who played a significant role over 1,300 years ago. He established Japan's system and greatly developed its culture, earning respect as a kami to this day for his remarkable achievements.

What Can You Ask Emperor Tenmu For?

Emperor Tenmu is a kami who lends his power to various wishes.

  • Stable Life: When you wish for stability in society or organizations, it’s a good idea to pray to Emperor Tenmu.
  • Growth in Studies and Culture: If you want to excel in your studies or deeply learn about culture and the arts, praying to Emperor Tenmu might bring you new knowledge and ideas.
  • Overcoming Difficulties: When facing challenging problems, praying to Emperor Tenmu might grant you the strength to overcome them.

The Great Achievements of Emperor Tenmu

Ritsuryō (律令): Emperor Tenmu established the "Ritsuryō," a set of rules to unify the nation, similar to modern laws. Thanks to these rules, society became stable as people lived under the same regulations.

A Cultural Hero: Emperor Tenmu also worked to harmonize Shinto and Buddhism and promote the arts. During his era, the Shikinen Sengu system, still practiced at Ise Jingu, was established, and many temples and cultural facilities were built, enriching Japanese culture. Emperor Tenmu laid the foundation for what is now Japanese culture.

Offerings to Emperor Tenmu

Appropriate offerings for Emperor Tenmu include:

  • Sake (Japanese Rice Wine): A special drink from Emperor Tenmu's time, perfect for offering.
  • Rice and Rice Products: As the staple food of Japan, rice is a fundamental offering.
  • Performing Arts and Artworks: Given Emperor Tenmu's deep respect for culture and the arts, offering artistic items is most fitting.

Emperor Tenmu is revered for his great achievements and leadership, being the one who compiled the "Kojiki" and "Nihon Shoki," and introduced the name of our country, "Japan," and the title "Emperor." By learning about his stories and roles, you can feel the power and charm of Emperor Tenmu. When you're in need or starting something new, praying to Emperor Tenmu might bring you strong kami blessings.
